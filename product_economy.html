Description

This is a Economy Skript to use on shop servers or any other kind of server
It can convert numbers into more readable numbers for example: 2000 becomes 2k, 2400 becomes 2.4k
This can go up until 1 Vigintillion which is a 1 with 120 following zeros!

Commands:

/pay (player) (amount)
/bal [(player)]
